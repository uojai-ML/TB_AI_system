AI-powered Tuberculosis Detection System

Every breathe counts

Approximately 14,000 expected cases Per 100,000 persons

Tuberculosis (TB) is a serious infectious disease that primarily affects the lungs. It is caused by the bacterium Mycobacterium tuberculosis and can be spread
through the air when an infected person coughs or sneezes. Early detection and treatment are crucial to prevent the spread of TB and improve patient outcomes.
This application uses advanced AI to analyze chest X-ray images and detect signs of Tuberculosis.

How it works

  1. A doctor gets a chest x-ray image from the patient.
  2. The image is uploaded into the model.
  3. In few seconds, the system will produce the results, whether there is TB or not.
  4. Doctor will then use the result to make the next decision.
