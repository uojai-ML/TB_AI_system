Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is this application about?

This application uses AI to analyze chest X-ray images and detect signs of Tuberculosis, helping healthcare professionals make informed decisions.

Who can use this application?

This application is designed for healthcare professionals, researchers, and organizations involved in Tuberculosis detection and treatment.

Technical Questions

What kind of images does the application support?

The application supports chest X-ray images in standard formats such as JPEG and PNG.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

The AI model has been trained on a large dataset and provides high accuracy, but it is intended to assist professionals and not replace medical expertise.
